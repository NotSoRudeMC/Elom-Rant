The first Problem: Predictability

(edited version)
I guess I'll start with Predictability and there being no reason to chat in Elom.
With this i mean to say that whenever i say something in Elom and sometimes even before i say something
I already know the responses to my message and all other ways that conversation could head in.
If I already know all the responses then there is no point in saying anything as that is akin to talking to an ai or even talking to yourself.
Elom has a sort of meta at this point. Every message has a default reply that being something that ends a chat like gg ez or something like snek to mess around.
Personally this is something that i dont particularly like. I'll take example of a video game to explain this further.
Now imagine you're playing an idle game( those games where all you have to do is click and idle ig.)
In this game you play it and grind a ton having tons of fun along the way, now after a few months of playing
you settle into a state where each thing you do is something you had planned beforehand.
You know exactly what You'll do and what will the reaction be and after a while of that you realize that
all this grinding and mundaness has gotten to put it bluntly boring and disinteresting.
This is about the time that you pack up and leave the game maybe returning to it once in a while
but as a whole you aren't playing that game and you would rather playing something more fun.
But surprise surprise friendships and friend groups especially don't work like that. Once a group reaches this state of Mundaness it dies hard,
speaking from personal experience a ton of groups i used to send thousands of messages in daily
i dont even check up on because of this reason aswell.
This also plays into why i decided to make this rant.
I dont want Elom to die in the same way that group died.
I've ranted a lot about this so how about I show some examples of this... oh wait i dont have to,
just read my messages about literally predicting what everyone was going to reply with maybe that will help.
The only way to get rid of this problem is to be original I know that may sound like a tall order but we have to try.
I dont want to have to try and keep something this dry and unrewarding alive.
